MAYHEM ROBOTICS

A community-based robotics nonprofit in Bedford, New Hampshire.

Mayhem Robotics is a registered 501(c)(3) organization that helps students ages 6–18 learn STEM and leadership through FIRST Robotics programs, classes, and hands-on outreach across Southern New Hampshire.

Our teams draw students from many towns and school systems. Whether a student is new to robotics or ready for advanced competition, Mayhem is a place where curiosity turns into creation.

Discover. Build. Compete. Have Fun.

From LEGO robotics to 125-pound competition robots, Mayhem offers programs for students ages 6–18.

Ages 13–18

FRC – Mayhem 8724

Our FIRST Robotics Competition team designs, builds, and programs a new industrial-scale robot each season to compete across New England.

Students learn mechanical design, CAD, programming, electrical, drive team strategy, and project management in a true engineering team environment.

Learn more
Ages 9–14

FIRST LEGO League Challenge

Mighty Mayhem FLL Challenge teams explore real-world problems, design LEGO robots to complete themed missions, and present innovation projects at events.

Fall season runs roughly August–December with optional spring sessions.

Learn more
Ages 6–10

FIRST LEGO League Explore

Our Explore teams introduce younger students to basic engineering, sensors, and core FLL values in a playful, low-pressure environment.

Programs emphasize creativity, teamwork, and fun through themed builds and simple code.

Learn more

Full STEAM Ahead

In addition to FIRST programs, Mayhem plans to run workshops and classes in CAD, 3D printing, basic mechanics, and programming. These offerings will support our teams and serve students who want focused skill-building outside of competition seasons.
